The Creative Act

A way of being that draws on the wisdom and experience of a legendary music producer to reveal the nature of creativity and show how anyone can more fully realize their creative potential.

The creative act isn't about receiving; it's about perceiving. It isn't about achieving a result; it's about going through a process. It isn't about taking in information; it's about opening up to an experience. It isn't about knowing; it's about wondering.

The creative act is a way of being in the world. It's a way of seeing, of hearing, of moving through space. It's a way of engaging with life that allows us to discover what we didn't know we were looking for.
